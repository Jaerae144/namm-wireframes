The live webcast, a collaborative effort between SupportMusic and the John Lennon Educational Tour Bus, is the first of several that will be taking place in various U.S. cities this fall. It will be a gathering of education leaders and elected officials who will share their concerns and ideas for expanding access to music and arts education as vital to a complete and quality education for all children.

Panelists inlcuded ...

  • Adam Bovie
    Director of Bands, Carsbad High School
  • Karen Childress-Evans
    Director VAPA, San Diego Unified School District
  • Dr. Merryl Goldberg
    Department Chair, Cal. State San Marcos VPA, Founder and Director of “Center ARTES”
  • Dalouge Smith
    President and CEO, San Diego Youth Symphony
  • Dr. Devin Vodicka
    Superintendent, Vista School District
